A0-A16	- needed: for PIA access only A8-A16 are necessary
D0-D7	- needed
E	- needed: clock
R/W	- needed: data direction (from/to CPU)
VMA	- needed: addresses PIAs when high
NMI	- not needed: connected to GND via switch
IRQ	- probably not needed
RESET	- probably not needed
BA	- not needed (bus available not connected)
EXTAL	- not needed (4MHz)
XTAL	- not needed (crystal unused)
